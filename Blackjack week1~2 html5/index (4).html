How It Works

  1. 01

    Place Your Bet

    Start a new round and decide how many chips you want to wager.

  2. 02

    Receive Your Cards

    You and the dealer are each dealt two cards. One dealer card stays face down.

  3. 03

    Hit or Stand

    Request more cards (Hit) or keep your current hand (Stand). Don't exceed 21!

  4. 04

    Dealer Reveals

    The dealer flips the hidden card and draws until reaching 17 or higher.

  5. 05

    Winner Declared

    Closest to 21 wins. Bust and you lose. Hit exactly 21 for Blackjack!
