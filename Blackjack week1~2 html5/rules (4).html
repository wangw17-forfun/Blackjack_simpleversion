Game Rules

Everything you need to know to beat the dealer.

Objective

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer by having a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer, without going over 21 (called a Bust). You are only competing against the dealer — not other players.

"Getting closer to 21 than the dealer, without exceeding it, is the single goal of every hand."

— Classic Casino Rule

Card Values

Card Value Notes
2 – 9 Face Value A 7 is worth 7 points.
10, J, Q, K 10 Points All picture cards are worth 10.
A (Ace) 1 or 11 Points Counts as 11 unless it causes a Bust; then counts as 1.

How a Round Plays

  1. Place Your Bet

    Before cards are dealt, choose your wager from the chip buttons on the game table.

  2. Initial Deal

    Both you and the dealer receive two cards. Your cards are face-up. The dealer shows one card face-up and keeps one face-down (the hole card).

  3. Player Actions

    You choose an action on your turn (see the actions table below).

  4. Dealer's Turn

    After you Stand, the dealer flips the hole card. The dealer must draw cards until reaching a total of 17 or higher.

  5. Determine the Winner

    Compare totals. Closest to 21 wins. Bust = automatic loss.

Player Actions

Hit

Draw one more card from the deck. You may Hit as many times as you like, but if your total exceeds 21 you Bust and lose the round.

Stand

Keep your current hand and end your turn. The dealer then plays out their hand.

Double Down

Double your original bet and receive exactly one more card, then automatically Stand. Only available on your first two cards.

Blackjack!

If your first two cards total 21 (an Ace + a 10-value card), that's Blackjack. You win 1.5× your bet automatically, unless the dealer also has Blackjack (Push).

Win, Lose & Push

You Win
Your total is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting. Payout is 1:1 (even money).
Blackjack Win
Your first two cards equal 21 and the dealer does not have Blackjack. Payout is 3:2 (you win 1.5× your bet).
You Lose
You Bust (total > 21), or the dealer's total is higher than yours without busting.
Push (Tie)
You and the dealer have the same total. Your bet is returned — no win, no loss.

Quick Reference

  • Dealer must hit on 16, stand on 17+
  • Bust = total over 21 = instant loss
  • Blackjack pays 3:2 (1.5×)
  • Push returns your bet
  • Ace counts as 11 until it causes a bust, then 1
